Puerto Princesa

Where nature begins and never ends.

Welcome to the enchanting world of Puerto Princesa, a tropical paradise nestled in the heart of the Philippines. With its pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and captivating underground river, this coastal city on Palawan Island is a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. Join us on a virtual journey to discover the natural wonders, cultural richness, and unforgettable experiences that make Puerto Princesa a must-visit destination.

Lore of Puerto Princessa

Historically, the place was named after Princess Asunción, born in 1864 to Queen Isabella II and her consort, Francisco de Cádiz. When the princess suffered an untimely death, the Queen changed the name to Puerto de la Princesa. Eventually, the name was reduced to Puerto Princesa as it is known today.

Weather Statistics

Get the latest weather information for Puerto Princesa to plan your trip accordingly.

In Puerto Princesa, the wet season is overcast, the dry season is windy and mostly cloudy, and it is hot and oppressive year round. Over the course of the year, the temperature typically varies from 76°F to 90°F and is rarely below 73°F or above 92°F. Based on the beach/pool score, the best time of year to visit Puerto Princesa for hot-weather activities is from late December to early April.
